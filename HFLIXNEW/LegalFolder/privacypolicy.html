1. Definition

HFLIX's data protection statement is based on the terms used by European legislators to pass the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Our data protection statement should be clear and understandable to the public, customers and partners. To ensure this, we must first explain the terminology used.

In this data protection statement, we specifically use the following terms:

personal information

Personal data refers to any information related to an identified or identifiable natural person ("data subject"). An identifiable natural person is a natural person that can be directly or indirectly identified, especially by referring to names, identification numbers, location data, online identifiers, or specific physical, physiological, genetic, spiritual, economic, cultural, or social identities of the natural person. Data subject

The data subject is any identified or identifiable natural person whose personal data is processed by the controller responsible for processing.

Processing

Processing is any operation or set of operations performed on personal data or personal data sets, whether through automated means, such as collection, recording, organization, structuring, storage, adaptation or modification, retrieval, consultation, use, transmission, or dissemination Or provide in other ways, align or combine, restrict, delete or destroy and disclose.
